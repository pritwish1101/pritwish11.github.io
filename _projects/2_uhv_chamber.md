The full ⁸⁸Sr experimental apparatus is modelled in SolidWorks as a single assembly of ~290 solid bodies — chamber, oven, Zeeman slower, 2D and 3D MOT optics, load lock with transfer arm (500 mm stroke), MDC viewports, KJLC vacuum gauge, Thorlabs cage hardware, and the photonic-crystal defect cavity at the centre of the main chamber.

I maintain the assembly and use it to plan optical access, check magnetic-coil geometry, and dry-run the build sequence on dummy samples before committing real devices.

**Stack:** SolidWorks 2024, OCP / CadQuery for STEP-file analysis in Python.
