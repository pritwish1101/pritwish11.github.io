A small library that wraps published atomic data for strontium-88 and provides convenience functions for everything I keep wanting at a glance: transition wavelengths, linewidths, saturation intensities, Clebsch–Gordan-weighted branching ratios, and Doppler / recoil temperatures.

Built originally for myself, but pip-installable and documented so others in the group can use it.

**Stack:** Python, NumPy, SciPy.
