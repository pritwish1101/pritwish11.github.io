Undergraduate thesis at the University of Delhi (advisor: Dr. Lovedeep Sahota).

We built and characterised a solar distillation system enhanced with carbon quantum dot, multi-walled carbon nanotube, and single-walled carbon nanotube nanoparticle additives, then developed a generalised thermal model coupling all the major heat-transfer paths. The model predicts purified-water yield as a function of irradiance, nanoparticle loading, and ambient conditions.

The work was published in the ASME Journal of Solar Energy Engineering ([doi:10.1115/1.4064774](https://doi.org/10.1115/1.4064774)).

**Stack:** MATLAB, experimental thermometry, peer review.
