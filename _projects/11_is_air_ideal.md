A simple home-built U-tube manometer experiment from undergrad: measure the pressure–volume relationship of trapped air at constant temperature and check how well it follows the ideal-gas law.

Air is closer to ideal than most people guess, but the small deviation from PV = const is real and measurable with kitchen-grade apparatus if you take care with calibration. A satisfying first experiment in being honest about uncertainty budgets.
