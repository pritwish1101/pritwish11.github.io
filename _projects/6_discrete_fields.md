My master's thesis at IIT Delhi (advisor: Prof. Sarthak Parikh).

Branching lattices — infinite trees where the bulk encodes a boundary CFT — are a discrete analogue of AdS space and a natural setting for studying holography without continuum subtleties. The thesis develops a new method to compute scalar-field propagators on these lattices in closed form, then extends the construction to broader classes of discrete AdS spaces.

The work was carried forward into a short post-master's research fellowship probing quantum-gravity corrections in the same framework.
